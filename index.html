BIO

I am Amal Chandh V, a postgraduate student of Economics with a strong interest in macroeconomics, public finance, international economics, and development policy. My work focuses on analysing economic trends, understanding policy impacts, and interpreting data-driven insights that shape the Indian and global economy.

Over the years, I have developed a growing passion for transforming complex economic concepts into clear, accessible knowledge. I regularly study and write about topics such as inflation dynamics, monetary policy, government budgets, innovation-driven growth, international trade practices, and the evolving structure of the Indian economy.

I am currently preparing for the Indian Economic Service (IES), with the aim of contributing meaningfully to evidence-based policymaking, economic research, and national development.

WHAT I DO BEST

  • Write research articles & policy notes
  • Analyse economic developments using data
  • Create visual economic dashboards
  • Assist students with notes and conceptual clarity

VISION

My long-term goal is to work in institutions like the RBI, Finance Commission, NITI Aayog, or the Indian Economic Service, contributing to research, policymaking, and economic strategy.

I aspire to build a platform that offers clear, reliable, and data-backed economic insights to learners, researchers, and policymakers.
